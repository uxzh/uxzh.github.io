Idea

Helping friends to gift and receive

As a child, my friends and I liked to make surprises, but sometimes it turned out that someone alone did not receive a gift because they forgot about it, or there were not enough gifts, or someone did receive several at all, and someone did not receive at all. This application, in theory, should solve this problem.

Concept

Christmas envelopes and Candy Canes were taken as a concept. Their lines are perfect for design.

Likewise, the element of the announcement of the person to whom you need to give a gift. It is sealed so that no one except you can find out who you are giving the gift to. Previously, people had to pull pieces of paper to randomly get the name of another person. Now, in a pandemic, a remote solution is needed.

Problems

My main goal was to develop design based on released applications in Play Store and App Store. Improvement of basic elements, user experience and color scheme.

Result

As a result of the work, colors, margins and text have been improved. The free space is used as intended.
